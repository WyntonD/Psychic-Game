By: Tim Weaver | October 28, 2018 3:39 pm ET

The Carolina Panthers are putting a beating on what was the NFL’s No. 1 defense coming into this week. Carolina’s diverse array of offensive weapons have all been on display in today’s game, including quarterback Cam Newton’s legs.

Watch Newton score easily on a designed run play to put the Ravens on the ropes in the fourth quarter.
